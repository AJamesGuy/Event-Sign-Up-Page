FAQs

  • What time does each event start?

    • Events will go from 9pm to 3am in the cities' local time zone.

  • When does admission begin?

    • General and student admission will begin 1 hour prior to the event. VIP admissions will begin 2 hours before the event.

  • What am I allowed to bring?

    • No alcohol or drugs will be allowed on event grounds. Water and fans are recommended.
